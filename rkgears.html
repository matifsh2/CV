Duties in RK Gears Pvt Ltd

• Preparing sales invoices & and keep record of A/R, detail of amount received and receivable aging list.
• Inputting of vendor invoices and keep record of A/P, detail of payments and vendors aging list. Ensure vendor payments in time.
• Preparation and input of month end journal vouchers.
• Preparation of Debtors, Creditors & Bank reconciliation.
• Audit of customer payment received and held; rectify discrepancies in accounts receivable books.
• Audit of deposits & payments made by bank and reconcile all payment/Receipts with bank statements.
• Maintain and manage day-to-day transactions record in Accounting System.
• Assist external audits and resolve queries.
• Preparation of various financial reports for senior managers to have updated information about business.
• Conducting regular business reviews of financial performance.
• Meeting with Senior Management to present reviews about how to make improvement in internal controls to be more effective and to overcome system weaknesses.
• Assist in preparing budgets, periodic financial statements and records.
• Assist in the preparation of monthly balance sheet & Profit & Loss Statement.
• Manage office expenditure within the budget and prepare Petty Cash book.
• Sales Tax Preparation and E-Filing.
